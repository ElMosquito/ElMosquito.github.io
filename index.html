Coat Colours

Complete list of all coat colours and their names. If no specific number (1 or 2) is specified for a gene in the drop down menu there is no difference in appearance
whether the horse has one or two copies of the gene.

If a specific coat has an image available there will be one or several squared buttons after the coat name:
Numbered buttons are linked to one or several examples of a specific coat.
Will show a picture of horses with the Tiger eye gene.
Will show examples of horses with the Metallic sheen gene.
Will show examples of horses with white markings caused by the tobiano gene, but not enough white to be called tobiano.

Free text searching:
When typing in the search box the list will show all the coats containing the word you write. In addition to coat names you can search for pictures of additional genes like
tiger eye, metallic sheen and white markings. If you want to show all the coats with a picture for tiger eye type tiger in the search box. The word metallic will show the coats
that have pictures of metallic sheen. You can also search for images of specific white markings like blaze, stripe, bald face, star, snip, stocking, sock, pastern and coronet.

Grey and Leopard patterns:
Since the list would become extremely long and slow to load the Grey and Leopard genes are left out from the list except for the basic coats. Both Grey and Leopard patterns
can be added on top of all the other coats in the list. The name for grey horses will always be Grey regardless of their original coat they are born with.
For any of the leopard patterns just add the name of the specific LP pattern to the end of the coat name (but before Tobiano if the horse also has tobiano markings).
You can search for any available pictures of grey and leopard patterns by typing their names in the search box. If there are available examples of Grey or leopard patterns on
any other coat than the ones you can search for in the drop down menus they will have either or buttons.
